I like to consider myself as a bit of a unicorn. Why? Because I was born and raised here in the Portland, Oregon! For twenty-eight years I've been swimming, hiking, biking, snowboarding, and driving all over the beautiful Pacific Northwest, and although I've moved around the area, I've never been too far from home.

Depending on the time of year I can often be found watching football or basketball in one of the many incredible local breweries here in town. (If you've got time, sign up for a tour with Brewvana. It will be worth it!)

I graduated from Clackamas High School in 2008, and Multnomah University in 2015. After working at a warehouse for two and half years, my career trajectory changed when I started at Epicodus in March of 2018. I chose to pursue front-end web development because I am passionate about creating websites that are not only functional, but are also sleek and user-friendly. I lovethe beauty of the Northwest, and it's that rustic style and sense of adventure that I bring to my development and design.
